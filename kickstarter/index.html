The Vision

What if your world played itself?

No more scrambling for NPC names mid-session. No more forgetting who the blacksmith married. Eon Weaver advances your world between sessions and gives you a reviewed change-set — not random lore. You approve every change.

⏩

Advance Time → Get a Reviewed Change-Set

Skip 10 days of downtime. Get a list: weather summary, market shifts, faction moves, rumors, and 2–3 adventure hooks. Accept, reject, or modify every change.

🏰

Keep NPCs & Factions Consistent Automatically

Party ignores the thieves' guild for 3 weeks? Their influence grows, guards tighten, a rival faction reacts. All tracked, all logged, all reversible.

📊

D&D 3.5 Native — Not Another Wiki

Most worldbuilding tools only help you organize. Eon Weaver also simulates. Full stat blocks, SRD integration, and rules-compliant generation. Built for an underserved edition with a passionate community.

✅ What the AI Does

  • Downtime simulation — events, arrivals, departures, and social changes
  • Downtime events and complications
  • Faction & settlement state changes during time skips
  • Rumors and hooks derived from world changes
  • NPC population generation with full 3.5e stat blocks
  • Relationship and family tree simulation

🚫 What It Does NOT Do

  • No AI-generated art or graphics — ever
  • No AI-generated maps
  • No replacing the DM or writing full adventures
  • No hidden changes — you review everything first
  • No rewriting established facts without DM approval
  • No setting-shattering events unless you enable them

Live

✅ Core Application

A working AI-powered living world simulator for D&D 3.5 campaigns — with deep character mechanics and world-scale simulation already built.

  • AI Population Generator — generate full town rosters with unique D&D 3.5 stat blocks, gear, and backstories
  • Time Simulation — simulate months of in-game time; relationships form, buildings rise, events unfold
  • World Simulate — run AI simulation across ALL towns in your campaign sequentially, with inter-town character migration based on residency
  • NPC Backstories & Relationships — AI generates rich personal histories; NPCs form relationships, marry, and have children
  • SRD Browser — integrated spells, feats, skills, classes, items, and equipment — all searchable and cross-referenced
  • Character Creator Wizard — 6-tab guided character creation with point buy, standard array, and race modifiers
  • Level-Up Wizard — multi-step leveling with multiclass and prestige class support
  • Spellcasting Engine — spell slots by class/level with bonus spells, save DCs, prepared vs spontaneous casting
  • Equipment & Inventory System — per-item tracking with equip/unequip slots, automatic AC calculation
  • Conditions & Active Effects Engine — all 3.5e conditions plus spell buffs with duration tracking
  • Combat Math Engine — auto-calculated attack arrays and AC breakdowns
  • Encounter & Initiative Tracker — drag-and-drop SRD monsters, roll initiative, track HP/damage in combat
  • Town Social Panel — factions with members & inter-faction diplomacy, incidents with participants & clues
  • Campaign Dashboard — world overview with calendar, town list, and aggregate stats
  • In-App Bug Reporting — direct Discord webhook integration
  • Dual-Tier AI Routing — smart cost optimization routes simple tasks to fast models, complex narrative to powerful ones
  • Campaign Rules Engine — XP scaling, death thresholds, relationship speeds, conflict frequency
  • Target Demographics — define exact race percentages per town
  • Buildings & Infrastructure System — manage and construct new buildings, handle population slots.
  • Town Statistics Dashboard — rich visual breakdowns of races, classes, ages, and building counts.
  • Homebrew World Rules Engine (28+ tools) — modify planar rules, resource frequency, magic levels, survival odds.
  • Admin Dashboard — direct management of AI token limits, API keys, backing structures, permissions.
  • Prestige Class Engine (3.5e) — full feat, skill rank, selection prerequisite checking and application.
  • Metamagic Automation Engine (calculation engine) — slot adjustment, damage multipliers, and action-economy for metamagic feats.
  • XP Growth Score System — track resident downtime achievements on an automatic passive grading basis.
  • Multi-Edition Support — integrated modes for 5e 2014 and 5e 2024 play testing.
  • Homebrew Content Engine — create custom races, classes, feats, spells, and equipment with per-user SQLite isolation
  • Feat Modifier Engine — mechanical modifier builder for homebrew feats with auto-calculated stat impacts (speed, saves, HP, skills, etc.)
  • Unified Character Editor — full tabbed creation wizard works for both new characters and editing existing ones
  • AI Custom Character Import — describe any character in plain language and generate a complete D&D stat block via AI in one click
  • Procedural NPC Generation — instant town roster generation without any AI credits for standard NPC populations
  • Portrait Upload System — click-to-upload character portraits with auto-resizing directly on the character sheet

FAQ

Common questions

Eon Weaver is built natively for D&D 3.5 Edition — a massively underserved edition with almost zero modern tooling. All character generation, stat blocks, CR calculations, and SRD data follow 3.5e rules. However, we've recently added Multi-Edition support hooks for 5e 2014 and 5e 2024 built right into the framework.

Eon Weaver uses a budgeted dual-tier AI routing system. Simple tasks (stat generation) go to fast, cheap models. Complex narrative tasks (events, simulation) go to smarter models. You bring your own API key from popular AI providers (many offer free tiers). Most operations cost fractions of a cent — a full month simulation typically costs under $0.10.

This is our #1 design priority. Every simulation generates a tabbed results panel — arrivals, departures, deaths, social changes, and events — that you review before proceeding. Your campaign's lore, house rules, and 28+ world parameters are injected into every AI call, keeping generation consistent with your canon. Everything is logged in a persistent town history you can scroll back through at any time. The AI is a tool you direct, not an author that replaces you.

No. Eon Weaver does not generate, use, or display AI art. The AI is used exclusively for systems.
